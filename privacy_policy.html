Privacy Policy for Glintsy

Last Updated: March 2024

Welcome to Glintsy, a playful, game-based educational platform designed to support children with autism in their cognitive development. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use our application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to provide and improve our services:

  • Parent/Guardian Information:
    • Name and email address for account creation
    • Password (securely encrypted)
  • Child Information:
    • First name and age (3-10 years)
    • Autism survey responses for category analysis (Sensory, Calming, or Interactive)
    • Developmental profile and learning preferences
  • Usage Data:
    • Game interaction history and progress metrics
    • Schedule preferences and modifications
    • Screen time usage (tracked through parental dashboard)
    • Learning progress and achievements
    • "Jump Back In" game history
  • Technical Data:
    • Device information (iOS 17 or later, iPhone compatibility)
    • App performance metrics
    • Connection status for offline functionality
    • App size and storage usage (6.9MB)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal data to:

  • Create and manage user accounts through secure authentication
  • Analyze survey responses using Google's Gemini Flash 2.0 to determine appropriate learning categories
  • Generate personalized game schedules and recommendations
  • Track and display learning progress in the parental dashboard
  • Provide offline functionality through CoreData local storage
  • Improve our AI-based recommendation system (using anonymized data)
  • Send important updates about the app and your child's progress
  • Enable real-time updates of schedule changes and game progress

3. Data Storage and Security

We use multiple layers of security to protect your data:

  • Supabase:
    • PostgreSQL Database for user profiles, survey results, and progress data
    • Secure authentication for sign-up/login
    • Real-time subscriptions for dynamic updates
    • Serverless functions for backend logic
  • CoreData:
    • Local storage for offline functionality
    • Fast in-app performance for recent games and schedules
    • Local caching of survey results and progress metrics
    • Automatic syncing with Supabase when online
  • Encryption: All sensitive data is encrypted both in transit and at rest
  • Regular security audits and updates

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to enhance our app:

  • Google's Gemini Flash 2.0:
    • Analyzes survey responses
    • Classifies users into categories (Sensory, Calming, Interactive)
    • Powers recommendation logic for personalized schedules
    • Provides adaptive learning experiences
  • Supabase: For secure data storage and authentication
  • CoreData: For local data management and offline support
  • SwiftUI: For responsive and accessible user interface

5. Parental Controls and Rights

As a parent or guardian, you have the right to:

  • Access and review all data collected about your child
  • Modify or delete your child's profile and data
  • Control screen time limits through the parental dashboard
  • Update survey responses and learning preferences
  • Export your child's progress data
  • Opt-out of data collection for AI improvements
  • Edit or adjust scheduled activities from the home page
  • Access the Resources page for educational articles and tips

6. Children's Privacy

Glintsy is designed for children aged 3-10 with autism. We:

  • Require parent/guardian consent for account creation
  • Do not collect data from children directly without parental consent
  • Maintain strict security measures to protect children's information
  • Do not share children's personal information with third parties
  • Do not include in-app purchases
  • Provide English language support only

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of any significant changes through the app or via email.
